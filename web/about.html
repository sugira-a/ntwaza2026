About NTWAZA

We're building the most reliable delivery platform in Rwanda — connecting customers, local vendors, and riders through technology that makes everyday delivery simple, fast, and transparent.

On-Demand Delivery

Order food, groceries, retail products, and everyday essentials from trusted local vendors — delivered to your door in minutes across Kigali.

Real-Time Tracking

Follow every order from the moment it's placed to when it arrives — live GPS tracking for complete visibility and peace of mind.

Local Partnerships

We work with vetted restaurants, shops, and independent vendors across Kigali — supporting local businesses while serving customers better.

Secure Payments

Pay with Mobile Money (MTN MoMo, Airtel Money), Visa, Mastercard, or Cash on Delivery. All transactions are encrypted and PCI-compliant.

Our Mission

To make everyday delivery in Rwanda simple, safe, and predictable — for customers who need reliability and businesses that need reach. We believe that fast, affordable delivery should be accessible to everyone, not just those in central areas.

How We Operate

Ntwaza connects customers with a network of verified local merchants and trained delivery riders. Orders flow through our platform in real time — from placement to vendor confirmation, rider assignment, pickup, and last-mile delivery with live tracking.

Our logistics engine is optimised for Kigali's roads, traffic patterns, and peak-hour demand, ensuring delivery windows that are realistic and reliable.

Built for Rwanda

NTWAZA is designed from the ground up for Rwanda's unique landscape — local roads, neighbourhoods, and real traffic conditions. We provide responsive customer support, dedicated partner tools for businesses, and a rider network that understands every corner of Kigali.
