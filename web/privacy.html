Privacy policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how NTWAZA collects, uses, and protects information when you use our delivery services in Rwanda.

Information we collect

We collect information needed to deliver your orders and provide support, such as your name, phone number, email, delivery addresses, and order details. We also collect location data when you enable tracking so you can see progress in real time.

How we use information

We use your information to confirm orders, coordinate pickup and delivery, send status updates, and improve service quality. We may use aggregated data to understand demand patterns across Kigali and other service areas in Rwanda.

Sharing and retention

We share only what is necessary with merchants and delivery partners to complete your order. We keep information for as long as needed to provide services, resolve disputes, and meet legal or operational requirements.

Security and your choices

We apply security practices designed to protect data against unauthorized access. You can request access, correction, or deletion of your information by contacting us.

Contact

For privacy questions, email hello@ntwaza.com or visit our contact section. We respond quickly during business hours in Rwanda.
